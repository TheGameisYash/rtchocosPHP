Common Mistakes to Avoid

? Using water-based flavor concentrates. Even 0.5% free water in a chocolate mass causes irreversible textural damage. No flavor justifies it.

? Adding flavor during conching. High temperature and continuous aeration will volatilize most of your investment before conching ends.

? Tasting in a carrier oil, not in chocolate. Flavor perception in cocoa butter at body temperature is significantly different. Always validate in the final matrix.

? Skipping shelf-life validation. A flavor that smells perfect on Day 1 can produce rancid or chemical off-notes by Week 8. Test it properly.
