Our Story

From Passion to Profession

RT Chocos was born from a simple belief: chocolate is both an art and a science, and everyone deserves to understand both. With over a decade of experience spanning NPD consulting, production leadership and chocolate education, we've helped thousands discover the magic behind every bar.

As a true bean-to-bar brand, we source cacao directly from Kerala and Karnataka, controlling every step from roast to wrap. Our workshops combine deep scientific understanding with hands-on craft, empowering home bakers, professionals and entrepreneurs alike.

Whether you're tempering for the first time or formulating a shelf-stable ganache, we meet you where you are — with warmth, expertise and a genuine love for chocolate.
